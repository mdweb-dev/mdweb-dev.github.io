About us

About Foxes Moon

Foxes Moon is a residential care home set in the restful surroundings of St. Ives, Ringwood. The busy towns of Poole and Bournemouth, in Dorset are within easy reach, as is the market town of Ringwood, Hampshire and the New Forest.

The home is well appointed, offering our Residents tranquillity and respite with genuine care and attention from all of the fully trained care staff.

The home itself has 31 bedrooms, all of which are well-furnished, well decorated single rooms. The majority of the rooms are ensuite for added convenience.

Life at Foxes Moon

Residents are able to enjoy the use of two lounges, with televisions and radios in each. Foxes Moon also has a garden room and a quiet lounge, perfect for reading, relaxing and resting.

Residents are encouraged to socialise with one another, and Foxes Moon hosts activity sessions to counteract any feelings of isolation and boredom. However, Residents are also given privacy and personal wishes are respected at all times.
