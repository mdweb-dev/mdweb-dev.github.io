Who we are

Why Foxes Moon?

There are many things that must be thought-out when considering a move in to a residential care home. Any care home that you are thinking about must offer genuine comfort, care and consideration, making it a true home from home.

Both of our care facilities provide personal and superior care to every resident; we take the time to identify an individual’s physical, psychological, cultural and spiritual needs thorough observation, communication and assessment, making sure that our interaction with a resident offer reassurance and comfort.

We encourage anyone considering Foxes Moon to visit us and judge for themselves the outstanding level of care on offer. We welcome guests seven days a week.
